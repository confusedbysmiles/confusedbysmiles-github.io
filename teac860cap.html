TEAC 860 Capstone Project

Project Overview

This capstone project explores the integration of artificial intelligence tools in educational contexts, with a focus on creating equitable and accessible learning environments. The research employs mixed methods approaches to examine both the quantitative and qualitative impacts of AI implementation in classroom settings.

Key research questions include:

  • How can AI tools be effectively integrated to support diverse learning needs?
  • What are the ethical considerations and practical challenges of implementing AI in educational spaces?
  • How do educators and students respond to and interact with AI-enhanced learning environments?

The findings suggest that thoughtful implementation of AI can significantly enhance educational outcomes when paired with appropriate pedagogical frameworks and ethical guidelines. This project contributes to the growing body of knowledge around technology integration in education and offers practical recommendations for educators and institutions.
